About Us.

Jubilee Events and Organising Company is a premium event management firm headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. We specialize in full-service event planning, catering, entertainment, logistics, and media production, making us a one-stop partner for timeless event experiences.

Founded with a mission to bring elegance and excellence to every occasion, our team combines years of expertise in design, coordination, and execution. From corporate galas and weddings to private celebrations and public festivals — we manage it all with precision and creativity.
